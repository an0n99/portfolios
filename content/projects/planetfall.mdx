Arcanium is a decentralised, P2P solution to a highly centralised Real Estate Market. Our vision is to enhance accessibilty and transparency in the real estate market through a P2P real estate market, where all properties are minted as NFTs. Furthermore, Arcanium introduces a novel concept that is only just being explored, P2P decentralised mortgages.
